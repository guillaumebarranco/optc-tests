Welcome on OPTC TierList Maker

You can make your own different Tier Lists for One Piece Treasure Cruise by category.
  • Legends
  • Rare recruits (not including limited like TM boosters, World Journey or World clash etc...)
  • Limited Rare Recruits (commonly known as LRR, the ones you can have from Ray shop ticket for example)
  • TM characters
  • Raid characters
  • Colosseum characters
  • Ambush characters
  • PVP LRR characters
  • Kizuna characters
From these different categories, you can make Tier Lists from all the ways you think about.

There are even different groups like "F2P characters Tierlist" or even "Premium characters tierlist".

A last Tier List even enables you to regroup absolutely all the characters from the different categories, the you can do whatever you want with it !

You could for example make a global PVP Tier List containing legends, PVP LRR, raids characters etc...

Last of all, don't forget that you can change the title of every of your Tier List and that you can create as much of them as you want !

Enjoy !

PS : Please note that this application is made for desktop browser

General Informations
  • Tier List title is editable
  • Tiers names are editable
  • Only in the last Tier, you can make a right-click on a character in order to delete him from the Tier List. You can then display back again the deleted characters using an action button. You can then bring back the characters inside the Tier List with another right-click.
Filters
  • On the Legends Tier List, you can filter by debut year.
  • On the F2P, Premium Tier Lists and also on the one that regroups all characters, you can filter by character's category.
Save, Loading and removal
  • You can save your Tier List any time in order to go back on it later or even keep it saved. Your Tier List is saved inside your own browser with the Local Storage.
  • You can save as much Tier Lists as you want.
  • Your Tier Lists are saved based on their title. A Tier List loaded then modified will be erased by current Tier List. If you save a Tier List, it will erase and replace the saved Tier List with the same title.
  • You can load a previously saved Tier List. Please note that this will erase the current Tier List if you haven't saved it before.
  • You can remove a previously saved Tier List
Share & Export
  • You can share a Tier List with a simple URL. Clic on the "Share" button and the Tier List URL will be pasted in your clipboard (just do CTRL+V then)
  • You can export your Tier List as a PNG picture
  • You can export your Tier List as a PNG picture and choose to hide the last tier from it
